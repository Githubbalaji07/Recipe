Quantity

Ingredient Quantity
Water 500 ml
Olive oil 2 table spoon
Pasta 250 gm
Pasta sause 100 ml
Vegeatables As per liking
Herbs &Spices As per liking

Prepration

1) Boil the uncooked pasta
In a open pot add salt and uncooked pasta to boiling water , cook until it becomes soft.

2) Drain thw water
From the boiled pasta pot,drainn all the excess water.

3) Saughte the Vegeatables
Chop the Vegatablesand add the chopped veggies to an open pan with 1 tb spoon alive oil.

4) Add the Pasta sause
Pour pasta Sause into the pan with the veggies.

5) Herbs and Spices
Add herbs and spices of your liking to the pasat sause.

6) Add Pasta
Finally ,add the boiled pasat and mix well!

7) Serve
The pasat is noew ready,Serve Hot!
